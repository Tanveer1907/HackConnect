# Project Overview

## 🎯 Vision
To build India's first verified hackathon collaboration network for college students.

---

## 🧠 Concept
Students register using official college email IDs.
They can:
- Discover hackathons
- Build strong cross-college teams
- Showcase their portfolio
- Get internship recommendations

---

## 🔥 Unique Selling Point
Intelligent team formation based on:
- Skills
- Preferred roles
- Hackathon experience
- Complementary skill logic

---

## 🎓 Target Users
- College students
- Hackathon participants
- Recruiters (future scope)

---

## 🚀 Future Scope
- AI-based team recommendation
- Reputation scoring system
- Company dashboards
- Cross-country expansion
